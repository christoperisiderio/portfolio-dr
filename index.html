ABOUT



Hi, I’m Christoper Isiderio — a passionate Computer Science student and
Graphic Designer with a love for both technology and art.

I enjoy building things from the ground up, whether it’s coding full-stack
projects like a Spotify clone in Flask, configuring development tools like
Neovim, or designing sleek visuals in Photoshop and After Effects.
My work blends logic and creativity, allowing me to move fluidly
between programming, design, and storytelling.

Beyond academics and design, I’m a gamer at heart — diving
into titles like Persona 4 and Valorant, where I aim to push myself
to a competitive level. I also explore photography, music, and motion
graphics, keeping my creativity fueled across different mediums.

I believe in continuously learning and challenging myself,
whether through coding complex projects, experimenting with
visual styles, or taking on new technologies like AI, IoT, and Web3.
My goal is to create meaningful, innovative solutions that merge
technology, design, and real-world impact.
